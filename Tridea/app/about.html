Who & Why

I am Lisa Winstanley, an Assistant Professor at the School of Art, Design and Media at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore and Tridea is my idea!

Tridea came from my desire to address the social divisions we are currently facing instead promoting collaboration and altruism. Tridea embraces social invites creative people from around the world to participate in an online form of the Surrealist parlour game, ‘The Exquisite Corpse‘. The Exquisite Corpse is a method of collaging numerous designs by several artists into one collaborative composition. A creative mash-up!

Tridea’s mash-ups will be undertaken digitally; requesting artists to submit their outcomes online virtually collaborating to produce one multicultural creative piece. The artworks created will be displayed on our online gallery and sold as art-prints in our shop. All profits will be donated to charity encouraging a philanthropic approach to creativity! Design with purpose.

Singapore Book Council (SBC) ABOUT SBC ​​​​​​

The Singapore Book Council (SBC) is an independent charity with IPC status. Established in 1968 to support Singapore authors and literature, SBC aims to Build Our Imagine-nation by developing creativity, imagination, original thought and empathy through writing, reading, illustration and storytelling. We are committed to developing the literary arts sector through our festivals, training workshops and courses, as well as book awards to recognise excellence.
