NOT SURE HOW TO CREATE A TRIDEA?

Don't worry, we got you.

Step 1
Bear with us for these 9 steps! There is a sign up link below after you have read these.
Step 2
Before signing up, you must agree to the project's T&C's and complete a teeny tiny survey (less than 5 minutes, we promise!)
Step 3
After signing up, you will be randomly assigned to a team via e-mail and be informed of which order you will participate in the Tridea (Artist 1, 2 or 3).
Step 4
When the team is assembled, Artist 1 will be given up to 24 hours to create and upload their share of the artwork. Artist 2 and 3 will be informed that the design process has begun as well. Think of this as a design relay race!
Step 5
Artist 1 will then upload their artwork placed on the given template with a title and a short description. Submission details will be made available via e-mail.
Step 6
Artist 2 will receive a notification via email when the first artist uploads their artwork and their 24 hours for creativity begins. 1cm of the Artist 1's creation will be made visible for continuation in the next design. Same goes for Artist 3!
Step 7
Before signing up, you must agree to the project's T&C's and complete a teeny tiny survey (less than 5 minutes, we promise!)
Step 8
After signing up, you will be randomly assigned to a team via e-mail and be informed of which order you will participate in the Tridea (Artist 1, 2 or 3).
Step 9
When the team is assembled, Artist 1 will be given up to 24 hours to create and upload their share of the artwork. Artist 2 and 3 will be informed that the design process has begun as well. Think of this as a design relay race!
