Study Information Sheet

Thank you for agreeing to participate in the Tridea Project.
Please read the following terms and conditions and check “I agree” before proceeding.

Principle Investigator: Assistant Professor Lisa Winstanley. School of Art, Design and Media, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore

Email: lwinstanely@ntu.edu.sg IRB reference number: 2018-07-022.

Facilitating production & publication of international, collaborative creative practice by fostering a culture of creative trust.
This is a research project being conducted by Lisa Winstanley, an Assistant Professor at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore.

You are invited to participate in an online creative research project which requires you to create one section of a collaborate artwork.
Should you agree to participate you will be one third collaborator and will collaborate together with 2 other randomly assigned participants. You will be asked to answer a short survey concerning your participation in this project, including some basic demographic information. This information will remain confidential and individual data will not be shared with anyone or used for any other purpose. Your data will be stored on a secure, online data management system which is password protected and only Lisa Winstanley in capacity as Principle Investigator will have access to this information.

On completion of the collaborative artwork the resulting creative artefact will be available to view in a public online gallery and you will be credited for your artistic contribution wherever your artwork appears, whether that be online or printed collateral. You are free to either chose to be credited using your own name or a pseudonym. You will be allocated a 24 hour period to complete your share of the collaborative artwork the commencement of which will be clearly indicated for your acceptance on the website platform. The resulting artworks will be available for public purchase via the public online gallery and all profits resulting from sales will be donated to charity.

Right to Refuse or Withdraw: Your participation in this project is voluntary. You may refuse to take part in the research or exit at any time without penalty. You are free to decline to answer any particular question you do not wish to answer for any reason.

Risks and Discomforts: There are no foreseeable risks involved in participating in this study other than those encountered in day-to-day life.

Benefits: You will receive no direct financial benefits from participating in this research study. However, your responses may help us learn more about creating a collaborative culture of creative trust and creative communities contributing to philanthropic projects. Your creative work will also be displayed publicly and have the opportunity to be printed and sold internationally for a worthy cause.

Anonymous and Confidential Data Collection:

SURVEY ONLY
Your survey answers will collated and data will be stored in a password protected electronic format. Identifying information such as your name, email address, or IP address will not  be linked to any specific answers and data will be analysed as a whole, with only the principle investigator, Assistant Professor Lisa Winstanley able to identify. Therefore, your responses will remain confidential. No one will be able to identify your answers, and no one will know whether or not you participated in the survey aspect of this study.

ARTWORK ONLY
Your name, as you specify it on the sign in page (online) or on the participant survey ‘D’ sheet (workshop), will always appear alongside your artwork contribution. You are free to use your own name or a pseudonym. The artwork you contribute will become a donation to the Tridea project and you grant Lisa Winstanley, in capacity of principle investigator of this project, copyright privileges to print and reproduce this artwork for public viewing and sale for charitable purposes, as deemed applicable, for the duration of this project.

Confidentiality of records: A customized secure back-end password protected data management system will store artwork and survey data at tridea.ntu.edu.sg
Artwork/survey data will also be downloaded and stored on a secure password protected computer and backed up on a hard drive which will be stored in a locked cabinet. Only Lisa Winstanley as Principle Investigator will have access to identifiable survey data. The general public will have access to LOW RESOLUTION visual representations of the submitted artwork via tridea.ntu.edu.sg and are able to purchase the artwork as high quality art prints, greetings cards and other saleable merchandise from said site. HIGH RESOLUTION artwork will be only available to Lisa Winstanley as principle investigator.

Personal Data:
By signing the Consent Form below, you (or your legally acceptable representative, if relevant) are authorizing (i) collection, access to, use and storage of your "Personal Data", and (ii) disclosure to, and use and storage by, authorised service providers and relevant third parties, whether located in Singapore or overseas, for the purposes of the study. “Personal Data” means data about you which makes you identifiable: (i) from such data; or (ii) from that data and other information which an organisation has or likely to have access. Research arising in the future, based on this “Personal Data”, will be subject to review by the relevant institutional review board. Data collected are the property of Nanyang Technological University. In the event of any publication regarding the survey results of study, your identity will remain confidential.
