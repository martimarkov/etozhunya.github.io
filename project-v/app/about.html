Introduction

Recently, PESSI has embarked on a Reforms Agenda to not only improve the Business environment of the Province by incorporating E-powered technology to bring achievability and viability to all its systems of services but also to extend its services to informal sector. This set PESSI apart from other ESSI’s by being a pioneer in setting standards of excellence by introducing state of the art technology for an integrated approach.
Reforms team, PESSI has been working diligently towards realizing its mission; “Reforms for Improved Systems” by developing an in-house, state of the art, innovative centralized monitoring Dashboard under its reform roadmap. This one of a kind monitoring system allows key stakeholders to determine functionality index of all our Health Facilities against pre-defined Key Performance Indicators, monitor monthly online Business registration and Contribution collection, monitor field tours of each field Director in real time, outline dengue activities, up-to-date status of domestic worker registration, interlinked Human Resource Management Information system, Online Complaint management system and showcase PESSI achievements on a single visually designed portal.
