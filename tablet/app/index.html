Help Center

  • + What is Tablet?

    Tablet is a pharmacy of the digital age. We provide you with all your medication needs, without you having to go anywhere. Think Amazon of your pharmacy.

  • + How do I send you my prescription?

    You can take a picture of your prescription on your phone, upload it through the order form and we will take care of the rest.

  • + How soon can you deliver my medications?

    We offer same-day delivery, so you can start using your medication right away.

  • + How much does this cost?

    Same day delivery service is free with Tablet. All you have to pay is the dispensing fee, similar to any other pharmacy.

  • + Do you deal directly with my insurance?

    Yes! We work with all insurance providers. Simply let us know your plan details, and we will take care of payments for you.

  • + When and where will you be launching?

    We will initially be launching in the Downtown Toronto area, and expand from there. We expect to launch at the end of 2019 - stay tuned!

  • + Where can I get more information?

    You can email us at info@gotablet.co, and we will be happy to have a more detailed conversation with you.
