  • What We Do

    Securing a graduate placement can be challenging. Ultimately, it all comes down to having an application that is nothing short of exceptional. Taking general advice from the internet, however, can only get you so far in this hyper-competitive and nuanced job market.

    We once found ourselves in the same situation. Fortunately, after much hard work – and a fair shot of disappointments, we managed to secure places in London’s most competitive companies.

    Now, our mission is to democratise access to vital information, enabling you to view the successful applications of those who have made it through the door of the most demanding and prestigious workplaces.

    For more information, visit How it Works

  • The Problem

    The biggest companies in each sector receive several thousands of applications for a role, rejecting 80% to 90% of these before the interview stage. In other words, the odds are stacked against your success. Crafting a prosperous job application and defying the odds is a real challenge that can cause frustration for many aspiring professionals.

  • Our Solution

    We noticed that the people whose careers advanced the fastest were the ones who had access to previous successful applications or inside tips and knowledge from their network. It simply became a connections game. In order to level the playing field, we have created the world’s first-ever platform for viewing previously successful applications.

    Are you going to waste your next opportunity to secure a dream role at your dream firm? Take control of your career ambitions, and progress in a positive direction by studying successful candidates’ applications, and tailoring yours according to your profile and ambitions.
