Historical Real Estate Data

We are pleased to be the only real estate firm on Galveston Island that compiles historical data on the local real estate market. Here you can find an archive of real estate statistics covering Galveston Island — both Island-wide and West Galveston only — over the years. Our residential sales reports track the dollar volume and number of sold transactions, along with the percent change compared to the previous year. We collect this data twice a year, during the first six months, as well as at the end of the year.
